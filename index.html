Rock-Paper-Scissors Bot

This bot tries to learn the best strategy for rock-paper-scissors using Counterfactual Regret Minimization: https://wwwf.imperial.ac.uk/~dturaev/neller-lanctot.pdf

Objective

Suppose we are playing RPS for money. Each player places a dollar on a table. If there is a winner, the winner takes both dollars from the table. Otherwise, players retain their dollars. Try to win the most you can.
